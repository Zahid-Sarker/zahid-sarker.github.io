Why?

There is a constant need for a regular supply of blood because it can be stored only for a limited period of time before use. Regular blood donation by a sufficient number of healthy people is needed to ensure that blood will always be available whenever and wherever it is needed.

Blood is the most precious gift that anyone can give to another person – the gift of life. A decision to donate your blood can save a life, or even several if your blood is separated into its components – red cells, platelets and plasma – which can be used individually for patients with specific conditions. How this works

This is an online blood bank management system. This can be used by hospitals, blood donation organizations or even as a national database to store information of blood donors centrally.

After signing up, user can register as a donor. The search result will show all the available donors based on the search criteria and donor's informations can be seen after selecting a particular donor based on the search result.
