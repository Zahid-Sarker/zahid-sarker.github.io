To draw:

  • Click the Pen icon to select the pen mode.
  • Press and hold the Ctrl key and move the cursor inside the canvas to draw.
  • Release the Ctrl key when you want to move the cursor without drawing.
  • Use the pen slider to change Pen size.

To erase:

  • Click the Eraser icon to select the erase mode.
  • Press and hold the Ctrl key and move the cursor inside the canvas to erase.
  • Release the Ctrl key when you want to move the cursor without erasing.
  • Use the eraser slider to change eraser size.

To clear:

  • Click the clear icon to clear the board.
